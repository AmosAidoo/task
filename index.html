Frequently Asked Questions
How long does the ReadyForWork Career Accelerator program last

Cummulatively, the RPWCA program lasts for six months. RPW fellows will spend six weeks in an intensive career coaching program which they will proceed to the second phase of paid internship and apprenticeship

Will I get a job after participating in the ReadyForWork Career Acceleration program?

We partner with corporates and high growth startups to curate the RPW accelerator. After the six-week intensive program, our partner will provide fellows with paid internship & apprentice opportunities, potentially leading to permanent jobs. While we provide these opportunitues to all ReadyForWork fellows, we cannot guarantee who gets a slot as the program is competitive and merit-based
